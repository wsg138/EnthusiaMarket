# Welcome to EnthusiaMarket

This page orients you to the market system in about two minutes. When you're ready for the hands-on tour, head to **[Your first 30 minutes](walkthrough.md)**.

## What is EnthusiaMarket?

EnthusiaMarket is the plugin that powers the player economy on EnthusiaSMP. It adds:

- **Stalls** — rentable market plots you bid on at auction.
- **Auctions** — competitive bidding for stall ownership.
- **Sign shops** — container-linked shops inside your stall to buy/sell items.
- **Rent** — ongoing rent payments to keep your stall.
- **Sell offers** — sell your stall back to the system or to another player.

## How the economy works

1. **Bid** on a stall at auction.
2. **Win** the auction → you own the stall.
3. **Pay rent** daily to keep it.
4. **Build shops** inside your stall to trade with other players.
5. If you can't pay rent, your stall enters **grace**, then gets **re-auctioned**.
